Request a Refund

On this page

Since we don’t have access to your App Store purchases or billing details, refunds are handled directly by Apple. Please use Apple’s “Request a refund” flow.

How to request a refund (Apple)

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.
  2. Find your Toku Budget purchase or subscription.
  3. Choose Request a refund, select a reason, and submit.
Refund approvals and timing are determined by Apple Support.
